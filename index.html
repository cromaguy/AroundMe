Introduction

Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology (KIIT) is a premier university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha. Founded in 1992 by Dr. Achyuta Samanta, it received university status in 2004 and has since emerged as a global leader in education, research, and innovation. The institution is known for its multidisciplinary approach, offering programs across engineering, management, law, medical sciences, and social sciences.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. KIIT embodies this philosophy by providing world-class education accessible to all.

Key Features

KIIT is celebrated for its outstanding infrastructure, academic excellence, and global collaborations. Some key highlights include:

  • A massive 36 square kilometer campus with cutting-edge facilities.
  • Ranked among the top private universities in India with NAAC A++ accreditation.
  • Over 30,000 students from across India and 65+ countries.
  • More than 195 international collaborations with leading universities.
  • Extensive research programs with over 130 funded projects.
